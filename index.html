Estimates Show Southern California Faces Largest Home Fire Risk

California has been a hotbed of wild fires over the past decade. Data shows that the five largest fires in California history have occurred since only 2018.

While the risk of wildfires to homes is a state-level issue, estimates show that southern California faces the greatest risk relative to the rest of the state.

Some counties in the northern half of the state, however, do fall into the higher risk category. These counties tend to be largely agricultural areas surrounded by homes and real estate. An example is Sonoma County, which is one of the largest producers of wine in the state.
